递归实现：

```lisp
(define (square-tree tree)
	(cond ((null? tree)
		    '())
	      ((not (pair? tree))
	      	(* tree tree))
	      (else
	      	(cons (square-tree (car tree))
	      		  (square-tree (cdr tree))))))
```

map实现：

```lisp
(define (square-tree tree)
	(map (lambda (sub-tree)
		(if (pair? sub-tree)
			(square-tree sub-tree)
			(* sub-tree sub-tree))) tree))
```
